# How to Start a Podcast in 2025
In 2023, my sister and I launched a podcast, Culture In Between, where we explore the unique experiences of people who have grown up in a culture outside their parents' culture. We were new to the whole process and had to do a lot of research to discover what was needed to successfully launch a new podcast. In this blog post, I’ll share the tools and information you’ll need in order to launch a podcast of your own.

## RSS Feeds
An RSS feed plays a crucial role in the distribution and accessibility of your podcast episodes. An RSS feed is essentially an XML file generated by your podcast hosting platform. This file contains important metadata about your podcast that services like Spotify and Apple Podcasts rely on to display your podcast accurately on their platforms. The RSS feed ensures that whenever you publish a new episode, it automatically updates across all platforms, making it easily accessible to your audience.
